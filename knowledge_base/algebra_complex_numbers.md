# Complex Numbers

A complex number has the form:

\[
z = a + ib, \quad i = \sqrt{-1}, \quad i^2 = -1
\]

Powers of \(i\) cycle with period 4:

\[
i^1 = i, \quad i^2 = -1, \quad i^3 = -i, \quad i^4 = 1
\]

---

## Modulus and Argument

Modulus:

\[
|z| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}
\]

Argument:

\[
\arg(z) = \theta = \tan^{-1}\!\left(\frac{b}{a}\right) \quad \text{(adjusted for quadrant)}
\]

---

## Polar Form

\[
z = r(\cos\theta + i\sin\theta) = re^{i\theta}
\]

---

## Conjugate

\[
\bar{z} = a - ib
\]

\[
z + \bar{z} = 2a \quad \text{(twice real part)}
\]

\[
z \cdot \bar{z} = |z|^2
\]

---

## De Moivre's Theorem

\[
(\cos\theta + i\sin\theta)^n = \cos(n\theta) + i\sin(n\theta)
\]

Example: Find \((\cos 30° + i\sin 30°)^6\):

\[
= \cos 180° + i\sin 180° = -1
\]

---

## Cube Roots of Unity

The three cube roots of unity are \(1, \omega, \omega^2\) where:

\[
\omega = e^{2\pi i/3} = \frac{-1 + i\sqrt{3}}{2}
\]

Key identities:

\[
1 + \omega + \omega^2 = 0
\]

\[
\omega^3 = 1
\]

---

## Triangle Inequality

\[
|z_1 + z_2| \leq |z_1| + |z_2|
\]

\[
\big||z_1| - |z_2|\big| \leq |z_1 - z_2|
\]
